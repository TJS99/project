Set Spotify Playlist

Change your Spotify playlist by following the instructions below:

  1. Log into your Spotify account on Spotify's web player (Requires Spotify Premium)
  2. Find the playlist you would like to add (make a new playlist and add/remove songs to it on your phone to avoid constant updating)
  3. Find the playlist's ID located in the URL
  4. Type the ID into the textbox below and hit the submit button

For Example: in the Spotify playlist URL here - "https://open.spotify.com/playlist/20K12yslgchif3E69cjAYz" the "20K12yslgchif3E69cjAYz" is your playlist's ID that you may type into the textbox below


Set Google Calendar

Change your Google Calendar by following the instructions below:

  1. Go to your Google calendar page
  2. Under "My Calendars" on the left-hand side of the page, hover over one of your calendars and click the 3 dots icon
  3. Select "settings and sharing"
  4. Scroll down to "integrate calendar" and click on the "customize" button
  5. Customize your calendar to your likings
  6. Copy the "embed code" located at the top of the page
  7. Paste into the textbox below and then hit submit


Set Outlook E-Mail

Change your Outlook e-mail feed by following the instructions below:
**Note: you may have to sign in every so often due to security time-outs**

  1. Go to your online Outlook e-mail page in your browser
  2. Copy the URL link at the top of the window
  3. paste your URL in the textbox below and hit submit


Set Weather Location

When loading the page initially, a pop-up will show asking for access to your location. Please select "allow" to have the weather widget present your forecast. If you see a red message bar appear above the weather widget, this means that you must refresh the page to try again. If you do not wish to have your location shared, just close the weather panel using the menu buttons.
